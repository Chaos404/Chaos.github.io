In this case, you are the data analyst of Luxura, which is an e-commerce company focusing on selling luxury fashion products. The store exclusively sells several globally-famous brands, such as Adibi, Balena, and Celinna. These famous brands are partnering with Luxura to specifically sell and market their products to the Indonesian fashion market, because their market share has increased 38% combined in the last 3 years. The increasing market share is competitive between them, because they have similar price range & product types. These three brands do not have any partner store other than Luxura. Because of this exclusivity, Luxura has a mission to prioritize either Adibi, Balena, or Celina marketing. Luxura hasn't decided about which brand to focus on. Yet, based on partnership fee data, Adibi has paid 80 million US dollars to Luxura in February 2023, which was the highest fee ever paid to Luxura. Miss Sofia, your Head of Data, is someone who believes strongly in data. She will believe anything, if it has numbers & meanings. In this case, all the bosses in the company wanted to prioritize Adibi because it paid the hiahest fee to Luxura, but Miss Sofia believes we need to take a look at the purchase performance first. She wants to learn from past purchase data, which has been handed-over to vou. The data consists of purchase information and identitv of each user who has made a purchase to Luxura in the last quarter of 2022 (3 months of data). Hence, Miss Sofia expects you to deliver insights on this matter before the companv makes a decision. To deliver the insights, first you need to understand the business problem and the data you have.
